Urgent notice

The 2002 Asian Benchpress Championships Changed!!

Host country: India, near NewDelhi
Date: 16th-18th , August, 2002


As I reported at the 2002 annual congress at Korea, APF did not received the invitation of 2002 Asian Benchpress Championships from scheduled host country UAE. We discussed this matter and came to next conclusions.
Our president, Subrata Dutta of India took responsibility to host the championships even though there are limited time allowance. He kindly proposed to host the championships in India and the congress approved the proposal.
As soon as he came back to India, he organizes the team and will send us the invitation and the detailed information of The 2002 Asian Benchpress Championships in India.


General Secretary of Asian Powerlifting Federation
Susumu Yoshida
2002.5.25
