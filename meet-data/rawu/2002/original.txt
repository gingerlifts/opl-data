 2020 TONY CONYERS EXTRAVAGANZA
The 12th Annual Tony Conyers Extravaganza was an overwhelming success thanks to
hosts Bill Beekley, Tony Conyers, and the great folks at CrossFit Jaquar and Strong Life
Tampa Bay!

WOMEN’S REVIEW

The ladies took to the platform in the morning session, featuring lifters from Beast Barbell,
Strong Life Tampa Bay, and “Girls Gone Strong” from Viera Charter School.

Girls Gone Strong youth lifters Ashley Polacek (66 pound class), Natalie Norcross (77
pound class), Gaby Buigues (88 pound class), Grace Stapleton (105 pound class), Eliana
Burrs (114 pound class), and Madison Hirsche (198+ pound class) were joined by teenage
lifters Evelyn Haner (181 pound class) and Emily Christiansen (198 pound class) in the
winner’s circle. These girls won their respective classes and shattered the Florida State
Record Books!

Leading the charge in the Open and Masters divisions were Lizbeth Panchame (105 pound
class) and Val Wolfe (132 pound class). Both ladies won their classes, captured Best Lifter
honors, and broke existing Florida State Records.

The 165 class saw some fierce battles. Nadia Warden’s 605 pound total topped the 165s,
followed by Emily Peterson at 555 and Mary Verardi at 545. Heidi Christiansen and Trudy
Daxon rounded out the class. Brandi Bennett posted the day’s biggest total at 765 pounds
to win the 181s, and teenage lifter Emily Christiansen took first place in the 198s.

Congratulations to all the ladies that competed, and we hope to see you at the 2021 “Girls
Gone Strong” Women’s Open on Saturday, March 6, hosted by the girls from Viera Charter
School in Viera, Florida!

MEN’S REVIEW

The men took to the platform in the afternoon session and did not disappoint! Teenage
148 pounder Jacob Dayan posted huge numbers to win both the Teenage and Open
divisions in the 148 pound class, and 181 pounder Israel Hernandez and 198 pounder
Tanner Hurst brought home Best Lifter honors with their 1125 and 1560 totals,
respectively.

Port Saint Lucie 181 pounder Tim O’Brien won both the Open and Masters divisions, while
Brandon Elias 220, 1555 total), Kyle Hnatkiewicz (242, 1560 total), and Jonathan
Sandomenico (275, 1435 total) posted big numbers to win their classes in the Open
division.

Congratulations to Beast Barbell on their first place finish in the Mixed Team category and
Girls Gone Strong from Viera Charter for capturing the Youth Division team title. And more
congratulations to all the Session Two competitors - we hope to see you on the platform
at the 2021 Florida State Championships on Saturday and Sunday, May 15 and 16, at Viera
Charter School in Viera, Florida!
