Contest Results
2019 Raw United Tony Conyers Extravaganza
Saturday, November 10th
Stronglife Tampa Bay / Crossfit Jaguar

Friends,

Thank you so much for supporting both the R.A.W. United ministry and the 2019 Tony Conyers Extravaganza. Below you will find the results of the competition. The Female Best lifters were Suzanne LaForge in the Masters division and Tiffany Mitchell in the open. Male best Lifters were Raymond Hodges in the Masters and Jacob Dayan in the open. It was a great day of lifting and many thanks to all of the volunteers. We could not have these competitions without their hard work and support. 

Tim, Tony and Bill
