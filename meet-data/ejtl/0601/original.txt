On March 18 and March 19, the Estonian Weightlifting Association organized the Estonian weightlifting triathlon in Kõrvüla.

In two days, 104 athletes competed. On both days of the competition, many Estonian records were set.

We would like to thank our supporters who helped the competition to take place properly: Liiwi Heliis, Robocop, Realsport, Sulemees, Powerlifting.ee, Fitness.ee, City of Kunda, Aleksandri Guesthouse, Wilde Irish Pub, Fast, AS Coca-Cola Drinks, Kunda Nordic Cement.

We also thank the people of Kõrveküla Sports Hall, led by local leader Margus Pärsik, for their help and hospitality.
