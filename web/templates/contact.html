Contact Information

Please address all inquiries to: updates@openpowerlifting.org.

For corrections to data, please include a link to the corrections, preferably from a federation-linked source. Video evidence is also acceptable in the case of federation error or omission.

If a meet is missing, and you would like it to be added, please feel free to send an email! If the meet is a recent meet in the USPA, RPS, or USAPL, there is a good chance it will be added within a week. Other federations are updated on a less frequent basis, as they require manual checking.

If you would like to contribute data, please send an email! Help is always appreciated. Every meet requires manual work, and there's more than enough to go around!
